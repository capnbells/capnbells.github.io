Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 25, 2020

This is the Privacy Policy of mobile game “Space Storm: Asteroids Attack” for Android devices (hereinafter – “The Game”). This policy describes how The Game collects, uses and shares your personal information. By playing The Game you consent to the collection of this information. We use this data to provide The Game s functionality and to troubleshoot, improve and optimise it for you.

The data we collect

We use third party tools provided by Unity Technologies to collect device information to enable personalized ads and gameplay information for analytics purposes. Collected data mainly includes IP address, device parameters, device identifiers, ad identifiers, country and region, events and actions within the game.

You can choose to prevent your device information being used for personalized advertising by changing the settings on your device or by opting out via the data privacy icon in ad window. You may still see advertising, but those ads will not be targeted using your personal information.

Users can also disable ads by making a purchase in the game settings. Payment (e.g. credit card) information is sent to payment processors who assist Unity. Unity’s payment systems are not designed to store credit card numbers.

By accepting this Privacy Policy and playing The Game and unless you opt out of personalized advertising you consent to us and our advertising partners collecting and using specified personal information to improve targeting and analytics so that we can show you relevant third party ads in The Game.

For more information please visit Unity 3D Privacy Policy page: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Age limits

In order to protect children s privacy we don t allow persons under a certain age to play The Game. This age depends on country of residence:

  • 16 y.o. – Bulgaria, Croatia, Czech Republic, Estonia, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Ireland, Luxembourg, Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Switzerland
  • 15 y.o. – France
  • 14 y.o. – Austria, Republic of Cyprus, Italy, Lithuania, Spain
  • 13 y.o. – Rest of the world

We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal data about anyone under this age. If we learn that we have collected such data we will delete it as quickly as possible.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically to make sure you are familiar with the latest version. Any new policy will be effective from the date it is posted on this page.
